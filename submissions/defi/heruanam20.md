Ideal Summary

zkDeFi Insight Feeds is a privacy-preserving microblogging vApp for DeFi communities. It enables users to share insights, comments, and votes with zero-knowledge verification—ensuring credible discussions without exposing identities. By combining private posting, reputation-weighted voting, and curated feeds, the app reduces spam and sybil attacks while fostering a trusted, anonymous environment for decentralized conversations.

Project Overview

zkDeFi Insight Feeds is a microblogging vApp built on the Soundness Layer that enables users to share opinions, comments, and insights about DeFi while preserving privacy.

All interactions (posting, voting, commenting) are verified with zero-knowledge proofs, ensuring authenticity without exposing wallet addresses or on-chain identities.

Key Features

Private Posting – Share content anonymously with verifiable proof of authenticity.

Reputation-Weighted Voting – Votes carry weight based on zk reputation, not wallet count.

Feed Curation – Content is prioritized by quality and proof-based reputation, reducing spam and sybil attacks.

Anonymous Following – Follow topics or authors without leaving a public trace.

Why It Matters

DeFi communities need trusted discussion spaces free from spam and manipulation. zkDeFi Insight Feeds provides:

Credible, reputation-backed discourse.

Privacy-preserving participation for all users.

A reusable framework for governance, polling, and community reputation systems.
