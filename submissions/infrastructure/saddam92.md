AIChain is unique because it makes AI predictions verifiable and trustless using zero-knowledge proofs on the Soundness Layer. Unlike centralized AI APIs, users don’t need to trust opaque models—they can verify computations on-chain. Developers gain a new way to monetize models, and dApps get access to composable, zk-verified AI services.
