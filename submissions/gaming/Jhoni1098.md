### Description
Duck Hunt is a blockchain revival of the classic duck-shooting game, enhanced with fair play guarantees using zero-knowledge proofs.
Each shot is validated through Soundness Layer, ensuring no cheating (such as auto-aim bots or fake results)
Players can earn points, collect rare duck NFTs, and compete in a global leaderboard.

### SL Integration  
Proof of Shot: Every shot generates a proof (hit or miss) verified on-chain.
Leaderboard Fairness: All recorded scores are validated with Soundness proofs.
NFT Rewards: Rare ducks or trophy drops are only granted if verified by Soundness Layer.
