# SkyTap - Balloon Rise 🎈

SkyTap is a simple browser mini-game where you control a balloon and try to keep it flying while avoiding clouds.  
The goal is to survive as long as possible and beat your own score.  

👉 Main repo: [SkyTap-BalloonRise](https://github.com/Akagamixyz/SkyTap-BallonRise)

---

## About
-> Tap anywhere on the screen (or click with mouse) to push the balloon up.  
-> Dodge random clouds coming across the screen.  
-> Score increases the longer you stay alive.  
-> Works on both desktop and mobile browsers.
