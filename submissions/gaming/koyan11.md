## Project
**Title:** Soundness Gaming Hub  
**Category:** Gaming  

**Description:**  
Soundness Gaming Hub is a vApp that lets users create, join, and track gaming tournaments directly on-chain. Players can register with their wallets, earn points, and climb the leaderboard.  

**Vision:**  
To build a decentralized gaming hub where every tournament is transparent, rewards are trustless, and players own their achievements.  

**Technical:**  
- Smart Contracts: Tournament creation, player registration, prize pool distribution  
- Frontend: React-based interface for joining games & viewing leaderboards  
- Backend: Minimal, only for off-chain indexing (core logic stays on-chain)  
- Integration: Uses Soundness testnet for contracts & wallet login  

**Timeline:**
