### Description  
QuestChain is a quest & reward-based blockchain vApp. Players complete missions (quests) to earn NFT badges or in-game tokens.  
The goal is to build a reputation-based game where every player’s achievements are verified on-chain with the support of the Soundness Layer.  

### SL Integration  
We will use Soundness Layer for:  
- **Proof of Completion** → players can prove they completed a quest without manipulation.  
- **Identity Protection** → ensures progress can be verified without revealing personal data.  
- **Cross-Game Reputation** → badges/achievements can be reused in other games connected to SL.  

## Technical
