## Problem Statement
Many community members enjoy casual games, but there are limited opportunities for real-time competitive interaction within the ecosystem. A simple and fun trivia game could drive engagement while showcasing blockchain fairness.

---

## Proposed Solution
We propose a Trivia Battle Arena game where players join a live quiz session, answer questions in real-time, and compete for leaderboard rewards. Game fairness will be secured by Soundness Layer integration to prove question randomness and scoring.

---

## Key Features
- Real-Time Multiplayer: Players answer questions together in timed rounds.  
- Leaderboard & Rewards
