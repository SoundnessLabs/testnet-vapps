### Description
Krazy Arcade is a fast-paced, web3-native arcade hub where players can compete, earn, and trade in-game assets across a variety of mini-games. The goal is to reimagine the classic arcade experience with blockchain-powered incentives, where every achievement, score, and collectible is owned by the player.

### SL Integration  
Krazy Arcade uses **Soundness Layer** (**SL**) for:
- **Fair gameplay verification**: SL ensures game results and scores are tamper-proof.
- **Identity & reputation**: Players’ skill levels and achievements are verifiable across games.
- **In-game economy**: Secure settlement for rewards, collectibles, and cross-game assets.

## Technical

### Architecture
Frontend clients interact with mini-games, which communicate results to a backend verifier. The backend leverages SL for score verification, asset settlement, and identity consistency. A lightweight wallet integration ensures smooth onboarding for players.

### Stack
- **Frontend**: React + Phaser.js (for arcade-style gameplay)
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express  
- **Blockchain**: SL + EVM-compatible chain for tokens
- **Storage**: IPFS/WALRUS for game assets and user-owned items

### Features
1. Competitive mini-games with score tracking
2. Player-owned assets (skins, boosts, collectibles)
3. Cross-game reputation system verified on SL
