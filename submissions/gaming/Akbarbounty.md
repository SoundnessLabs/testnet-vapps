### Description
BattleArena is a multiplayer blockchain game where players can fight in arenas, earn NFT-based rewards, and level up characters with verifiable proof of ownership. It combines competitive gameplay with secure, tradeable in-game assets.

### SL Integration  
BattleArena uses Soundness Layer (SL) to verify ownership of characters, weapons, and arena rewards. SL ensures that all battles and reward distributions are backed by zk-proofs, preventing fraud and duplicate claims.
