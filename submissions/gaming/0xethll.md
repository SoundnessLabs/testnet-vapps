# vApp Submission: Sound Clicker - The Most Pointless Game Ever

## Description

A hilariously simple idle clicker game where you click to generate "Sound Coins" that make actual sound effects. The twist? Every click is verified on Soundness Layer for absolutely no good reason except because we can. It's blockchain gaming at its most gloriously unnecessary.

## SL Integration

Uses Soundness Layer to store click counts, verify achievements, and create NFT certificates for ridiculous milestones like "Clicked 1000 Times While Eating Pizza" or "Most Clicks During a Full Moon". Each sound effect is minted as an NFT because why not make clicking sounds collectible?
