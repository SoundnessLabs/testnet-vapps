### Description
RealmQuest is a blockchain RPG where players explore, battle, and trade items securely across a decentralized marketplace.
Problem solved: Traditional Web2 games don’t give players real ownership of assets, and current Web3 games lack secure cross-game identity.

### SL Integration  
Soundness Layer provides verifiable item ownership.

SL attestation ensures items are authentic (no duplicates/hacks).

Cross-game identity system powered by SL to let players use the same identity in multiple games.

## Technical
