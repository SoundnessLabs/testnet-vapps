### Description
Modern professionals and teams are drowning in unstructured data and fragmented workflows. They waste significant time and mental energy on:

Context Switching: Constantly jumping between different apps (browser, notes, documents, project management tools) to gather information and complete a single task.

Information Silos: Critical data is trapped in different formats—lengthy PDFs, dense web articles, meeting notes, and email threads—making it hard to synthesize and use effectively.

Cognitive Overload: The sheer volume of information makes it difficult to focus, extract key insights, and generate new ideas without getting distracted.

In short, the problem is a lack of a centralized, intelligent workspace that adapts to how you work, instead of forcing you to adapt to it.

### SL Integration  
The SL isn't a single step; it's integrated throughout the entire interaction cycle:

Input Analysis & Pre-Processing: Before any AI model processes the user's request, the SL scans the input (query, uploaded document, pasted text).

Output Validation & Post-Processing: After the AI generates a response, the SL analyzes it before it's presented to the user.

Continuous Monitoring: The SL monitors the overall system performance and user interactions for emerging patterns or new edge cases.
