### Description
CreativeChain is a social platform for creating and solving creative challenges. Submissions are verified via Soundness Layer zk-proofs to ensure uniqueness and prevent duplicates. Users can upvote, comment, and compete on leaderboards.

### SL Integration
Use Soundness Layer for zk-proof verification of submissions and on-chain recording of proof IDs to guarantee authenticity.
