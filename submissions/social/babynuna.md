### Description
What problem does your vApp solve? What does it do?
To find the right solution, every social interaction (such as posts, comments, votes, or community participation) can be verified through the Soundness Layer. This way, developers can securely, privately, and reliably prove their reputation and engagement as a real person without having to reveal all their personal data.
### SL Integration
