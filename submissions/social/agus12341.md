# vApp Proposal

## Title
**zkSocial – Privacy-Preserved Social Feed**

## Category
**social**

## Author
**@Agus12341**

## Summary
zkSocial is a Web3 microblogging platform built on the Soundness Layer that enables users to share content, comments, and votes while preserving privacy through zero-knowledge proofs.

## Problem
Traditional social platforms face major issues: user data collection, censorship, and difficulty distinguishing real identities from bots. On the other hand, many Web3 social projects are too open and lack privacy control.

## Solution
zkSocial introduces a new paradigm for decentralized social networking:
- Posts & comments can be verified without revealing the real identity (zkProof-based).
- Users build on-chain reputation tied to zkID, not personal data.
- Content validation is driven by staking & reputation scores, not opaque algorithms.
- Social interactions (like, follow, vote) are transparently stored on the Soundness Layer.

## Technical Architecture
- **Frontend**: Next.js + wallet connect (MetaMask, OKX, etc).
- **Backend**: zkApps on Soundness Layer to verify social interactions.
- **Privacy Layer**: zkSNARKs to hide real identity while keeping interactions valid.
- **Storage**: IPFS/Arweave for decentralized content hosting.

## Timeline
- Week 1–2: Architecture design & zkProof research for reputation.
- Week 3–4: Deploy base contracts (post, comment, like).
- Week 5–6: Integrate zkProof & on-chain reputation.
- Week 7: Testnet trial with community participation.
