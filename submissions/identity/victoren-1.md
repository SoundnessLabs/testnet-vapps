### Description
What problem does your vApp solve? What does it do?

Social media today is noisy.
Big names and influencers drown out real ideas.
We judge posts by who wrote them, not what they say.
That kills good debates and slows down innovation.

But what if the focus was only on the idea?
No names. No clout. Just content.

That’s where Echo+ comes in.
It’s a decentralized app where you share content without revealing your identity.

Built on the Soundness Labs testnet, it uses zero-knowledge proofs (zkProofs) to prove your content is real—without showing who you are.


Why It Matters

Right now, we follow celebrities instead of thinking for ourselves.

Projects crash because hype wins over logic.

We need a place where ideas stand alone.


This platform makes that possible. Everyone’s content gets judged equally, whether you’re Vitalik or a total beginner.


How It Works (Super Simple)

1. Post your content. The system verifies it’s yours with zkProofs.
2. Your idea shows up in a topic feed (like DeFi, NFTs, DAOs). No names, no bias. Just topic.
3. The community debates, upvotes, and refines the idea.
4. You can reveal yourself later or stay anonymous.
5. Earn rewards for quality contributions.
