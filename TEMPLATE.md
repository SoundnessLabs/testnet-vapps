### Description
What problem does your vApp solve? What does it do?
❌ Problem

Accessing the AI economy is complex and limited.

Running AI models requires expensive GPUs and infrastructure.

Investors have no easy way to earn yield from AI compute power.

DeFi lacks AI-native assets that are both stable and yield-generating.

✅ Solution (What the vApp Does)

Our vApp bridges AI infrastructure with DeFi by:

Tokenizing GPU-backed compute power into yield-bearing assets.

Introducing AID (AI Dollar) – a synthetic stablecoin backed by AI-driven yields.

Offering users a simple interface to:

Stake into AI compute pools

Mint AID

Earn sustainable, real-world AI-powered yield

In short: We make the AI economy investable for everyone through DeFi.
