## Project Name
MarioVerse: Decentralized Platformer Adventure

## Overview
MarioVerse is a decentralized 2D platformer inspired by classic Super Mario games, where players explore levels, collect tokens, and battle enemies. By integrating the Soundness Layer (SL) testnet, all in-game items, achievements, and player stats are **verifiably owned** and **securely recorded**. Players can trade rare items, stake tokens for rewards, and build a reputation in a trustless gaming environment.

## Problem Statement
Traditional platformers do not give players true ownership of collectibles or achievements. MarioVerse leverages SL’s zkApps and identity features to solve this, ensuring player assets are fully decentralized, verifiable, and tradable.

## Technical Architecture
- **SL zkApps**: Handle verifiable in-game transactions, NFT collectibles, and achievements using zero-knowledge proofs.  
- **Identity Layer**: Each player has a verified identity and reputation, enabling trustless multiplayer interactions.  
- **Smart Contracts**: Manage the in-game economy, token staking, rare item auctions, and leaderboards.  
- **Client Integration**: Web and mobile clients interact with SL testnet nodes via SDK/API for real-time gameplay and asset updates.  

## Gameplay Features
1. **Collectible NFTs**: Coins, power-ups, and special items are minted as verifiable NFTs.  
2. **Staking & Rewards**: Players stake tokens to unlock rare levels or earn bonuses.  
3. **Leaderboard Reputation**: Player progress and achievements are verified on-chain for fair ranking.  
4. **Multiplayer Challenges**: Compete with other players in trustless, verifiable game events.
